About Hieronymus Bosch

Hieronymus Bosch was a Dutch painter and draughtsman from the 15th and 16th centuries. He is known for his fantastical and often disturbing imagery, as well as his use of symbolism and metaphor. His most famous works include "The Garden of Earthly Delights" and "The Temptation of Saint Anthony."
